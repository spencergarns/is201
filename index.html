The Ultimate Surfing Experience

Whether you know absolutely nothing about surfing or you're a master of the sport, Spencer has a class to take you to the next level.

























Lessons

Master Class

This is a class for surfers who truly want to become the best of the best. This is the class that Sterling Spencer took before he betrayed me and created his own "master class". Needless to say, this class will teach you how to become the next world champion, even if Sterling is still on tour.

Tandem Lessons

If you've been getting bored of surfing alone, then this is the class for you. Tandem surfing includes the benefits of extra paddle power and endless laughs. Not to mention, it's a great activty for couples looking to spice up their relationship. Caution: DO NOT sign up if you're afraid to get up close and personal with your partner.

"Falling With Style" Class

Let's just admit it. We all fall now and again. So, you may as well learn how to do it in style. We know that you'll be pleased with the dozens of falling techniques that we've developed over the last eight years. We'll even take you out to second reef Pipe so you can practice your double frontflip over the falls bellyflop.
